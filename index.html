SpkEazy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2025

1. No Personal Data Collected

SpkEazy does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No login or account is required.

2. Microphone Access

Microphone access is used only for real-time voice translation. Audio is not stored.

3. Internet Usage

Audio is temporarily sent to cloud services (e.g., OpenAI) for processing. We do not store any audio on our servers.

4. No Ads or Tracking

SpkEazy contains no ads and no analytics or third-party tracking tools.

5. Age Restrictions

The app is intended for users aged 13 and older.

6. Contact

For questions, contact us at: legacycodes@outlook.com
